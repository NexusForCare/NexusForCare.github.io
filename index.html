What is Minerva Insight and how does the AI-powered UX research assistant work?

Minerva Insight is an advanced AI-powered UX research platform that guides you from research setup to actionable results. Using state-of-the-art AI and an intuitive interface, it makes every stage of your UX research—from survey creation to data analysis—seamless and insightful. Please note: our platform uses AI to assist and analyze—not moderate—your interviews, ensuring the natural interaction between researcher and participant is preserved.

How does Minerva Insight support the entire research workflow?

Minerva Insight simplifies complex research workflows. You can create a study, generate tailored surveys based on your project goals and admin prompts, and easily schedule or host voice/video interviews. The AI assistant helps seamlessly implement your study requirements, guiding both researchers and participants for more focused and accurate results—even when conducting interviews with voice or video.

How does Minerva Insight ensure my data’s privacy and security?

Data privacy is our top priority. You can choose to deploy Minerva Insight on your own secure server for full control, or use our cloud solution, which is built on leading privacy and security compliance standards. All data is encrypted and handled with strict access controls to meet enterprise and academic requirements.

Can I use only one or a selection of Minerva Insight's features for my research?

Yes, you have full flexibility to choose and use only the features that fit your research needs. Whether you require just survey generation, only heatmap analytics, or want to run AI-assisted interviews, Minerva Insight allows you to customize your workflow and focus on the tools that matter most for your study.
